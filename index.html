Who We Are

We are a group of students from University of Washington who were made aware of the challenges that the gender binary poses for people through a presentation given in our Informatics 200 class. Currently, the gender binary is the most dominant depiction of gender in society. However, not everyone identifies with these binaries, thus causing conflict within themselves on how to express themselves. People who don’t adhere to the gender binary have a hard time expressing themselves for fear of being judged or discriminated against. We wanted to do something about this issue.

Our Goal

We want to educate the public about the gender spectrum. This would make them less judgemental towards those who don’t adhere to the binary system. Along with them being less judgmental, we’d want others to feel comfortable with themselves. We want the non-binary genders to express themselves freely.

We want to bring awareness to the problem that the gender binary creates in society, whilst also providing support for those who are just beginning to transition or even question themselves outside of the parameters of the binary. We are attempting to remove the impact of stigmas on trans/questioning individuals, and thus enable themselves to live their lives with fewer perceived restrictions. By better educating people about the gender spectrum, we can help mitigate the cycle of inequality against those who do not adhere to the gender binary. Furthermore, we want to provide trans/questioning people with support in the form of online resources as well as tangible evidence of support.

Receive the Gift of Comfort

If you feel as if you could benefit from our help, don’t be afraid to request a package. After a short questionnaire designed to give us an idea of what to send you, we request that you provide your name, email address, shipping address, and phone number. After submitting the form, you will get a confirmation pin via text message that you will have to enter in order to confirm your request. The gift will contain several pieces of clothing for you. If you have any special requests, such as specific types of clothing you want, feel free to describe them in the textbox below.
